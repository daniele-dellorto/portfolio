Saiver

Visual identity

Interaction design

2020

Saiver is a company specialised in conditioning systems that operates worldwide. They needed a complete renewal of their visual identity, as well as a new website - which was built from concept to code.

The identity system reflects the double nature of the company, on one side extremely technical while on the other light and dynamic. The website translates the identity pillars into structure and interactions, providing an organised representation of people, products and processes involved. To strengthen the look of the brand, a custom display typeface was also designed.
