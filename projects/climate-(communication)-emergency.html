Climate (Communication) Emergency

Data visualisation

Editorial design

2023

“Climate (Communication) Emergency” is a master thesis’ project which attempts to depict the state of the art of climate crisis communication in the online weather forecasts’ environment. Weather websites and platforms have the opportunity to build a shared understanding of the issue through punctual and meaningful accounts of its concrete impacts.

Through the analysis of case studies in a digital methods’ approach, tendencies and recurring imaginaries are detected, contextualised and mapped. The result is a printed report following three distinct but interconnected research protocols, corresponding to different communicative contexts.
