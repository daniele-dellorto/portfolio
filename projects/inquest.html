Editorial design

2021

Videogames’ monetization has become more and more predatory over the years, exploiting behavioural psychology to lure people into spending money. However, most governments in the world are still reluctant to address these systems as gambling, as well as to take measures against them. Inquest is an independent magazine that analyses the subtle mechanics developed by the industry, for the purpose of making the consumers aware.

The series is planned to be developed in four issues, each one addressing a specific predatory mechanism in its different aspects and manifestations. Issue 0, fully designed and printed, focuses on loot boxes.
