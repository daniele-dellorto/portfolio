HEX

Game design

Interaction design

2020

HEX is a speculative project meant to investigate the relationship between superstition and death. It consists of an alternate reality game with digital and physical touchpoints interconnected and spread throughout the environment.

The experience starts from an interactive installation made of steel, wires and screens: on the main display, an evil eye looks around and curses people. Engaged in a “deathly dangerous” game, the users have to pass 3 tests about superstition to free themselves. The goal of the project is to make people reflect about the contradictions of superstitious beliefs.
