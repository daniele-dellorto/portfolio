If Lost Please Return To

Editorial design

Photography

2021

The relationship we have with our wallet is anything but pragmatic. We use it to store everything that has value, and not just in economic terms. Its content, therefore, can reveal a lot of things about who owns it.

What does your wallet tell about you?

Ten friends were asked this question, ten answers were collected and then all of them were invited to show the actual content of their wallets. The result is “If lost please return to”, an editorial and photographic project that portrays people through their belongings.
