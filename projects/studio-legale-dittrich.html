Visual identity

Interaction design

2022

Studio Legale Dittrich is a boutique law firm situated in the heart of Milan. They needed a new visual identity and a showcase site, reflective of both the company’s soul and the professional staff’s expertise.

The identity is elegant but neat, and is built around the core values of the firm: dedication, reliability, fairness and respect. The website, expressive of the visual system, is conceived as a statement to strengthen Studio Legale Dittrich’s image and as a way to expand the customer base on an international level.
